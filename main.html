CHAPTER 1

I find myself stranded in a small blue planet.

Jay: Where am I?

Rob: You are in Planet Terus.

Rob: Before you ask, the USA and every other country has been destroyed.

Jay: What year is it?

Rob: 2101.

Jay: Wow.

Jay: How did I get here?

Rob: You died in 2040 due to a heart attack.

Jay: And who are you?

Rob: I'm Rob.

Jay: L-like Rob from Gumball?!? That Rob?!?

Rob: No, I am the guy who died with you.

Jay: Also, what's my ethnicity? I never took a DNA test.

Rob: 60% Italian, 30% French.

Jay: Cool.

Jay: What is Planet Terus?

Rob: A place after the whole Earth was destroyed in June 5th, 2060. You'll see a lot of fictional characters here.

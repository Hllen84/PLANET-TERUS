CHAPTER 1: STRANDED

I find myself stranded in a small blue planet.

Jay: Where am I?

Rob: You are in Planet Terus.

Rob: Before you ask, the USA and every other country has been destroyed.

Jay: What year is it?

Rob: 2101.

Jay: Wow.

Jay: How did I get here?

Rob: You died in 2040 due to a heart attack.

Jay: And who are you?

Rob: I'm Rob.

Rob: I am the guy who died with you.

Jay: Also, what's my ethnicity? I never took a DNA test.

Rob: 60% Italian, 30% French, 10% British.

Jay: Cool.

Jay: What is Planet Terus?

Rob: A place after the whole Earth was destroyed in June 5th, 2060. We're the only people here.

Rob: You were the spectator of all the madness on Earth.

Rob: The AI boom. The Middle-Eastern conflict. Everything.

Jay: Where's everyone else?

Rob: They were too boring so they got erased from existence.

Rob: Also, this isn't just a blue planet.

The planet around me turns into my homeland.

Jay: That's cool.

Jay: So it's just a liminal space?

Rob: Yes.

Jay: Cool.
